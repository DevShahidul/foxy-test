01 CHALLENGE

In a time when assessors and appraisers are stretched thin, automating repetitive tasks to reallocate time to more important functions and reduce overhead is paramount.

As part of the home data collection process, property tax assessors assign a “Quality Class”, to each home. While Quality Class scales may vary, in this case the county uses a scale of 1-10.

Faced with the need to verify the quality class on tens of thousands of homes, this Texas assessing department turned to Foxy AI.

02 SOLUTION

Using photos of homes labeled with the Quality Class provided by county assessors, Foxy AI built a “Quality Score” model to verify assessor-scored quality classes on photos of tens of thousands of parcels in the county.

By automating the property quality classification for local municipalities, we help tax assessors and appraisers use their valuable time more effectively, reduce costs, and improve quality assurance.
